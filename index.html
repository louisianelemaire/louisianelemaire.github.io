I am a postdoc researcher in mathematical neuroscience at the Inria Branch at the University of Montpellier, in the MathNeuro team.

I part of the 2MDS project on the multiscale modeling of Dravet syndrome.

Research interests

I study how changes at the level of individual neurons (ion channels mutation, activity-induced fluctuations in ion concentrations) affect their excitability, and the consequences on neuronal networks.

Tools:

  • - Bifurcation theory, multiple-timescale dynamics
  • - Conductance-based and integrate-and-fire neuron models
  • - Model reduction
  • - Markov models of ion channels
