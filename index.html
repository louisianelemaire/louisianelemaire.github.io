I am a postdoc researcher in computational neuroscience at the Institute for Theoretical Biology at Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin.

I work in the Computational Neurophysiology group of Susanne Schreiber.

Research interests:

  • - Bifurcation theory
  • - Saddle-node loop bifurcation
  • - QIF neuron with potassium dynamics
  • - Slow-fast dynamics
  • - Conductance-based models with dynamic ion concentrations
  • - Mutations of the NaV1.1 channel (hemiplegic migraine and epilepsy)
